Services

Compassionate CNA-led care in a safe, dignified home setting.

Rahwa Elderly Care is operated by Rahwa, a Certified Nursing Assistant with hands-on caregiver experience supporting seniors with daily living, comfort, routines, and safety. Specific care needs are discussed directly with families to confirm fit and current availability.

01Core Care

24-Hour Care & Supervision

Ongoing support in a residential AFH setting, with attention to senior needs, mobility limitations, comfort, and daily safety routines.

02Core Care

Assistance with ADLs

Gentle, respectful help with bathing, dressing, grooming, toileting, mobility, and other everyday routines.

03Core Care

Medication Assistance

Medication reminders and routine support as appropriate for each resident's care plan, family direction, and provider instructions.

04Core Care

Personalized Care Plans

Care routines are shaped around each resident's needs, preferences, habits, comfort, and family input.

05Core Care

Chronic Condition Support

Daily awareness and supportive routines for residents managing needs such as diabetes, hypertension, arthritis, and mobility challenges.

06Rooms & Home

Private & Shared Rooms

Rahwa Elderly Care offers 4 private rooms and 2 shared rooms, giving families flexible options based on comfort, care needs, and availability.

Lifestyle & wellness

Daily life supported with warmth, structure, and comfort.

The home is designed for practical support, familiar routines, and meaningful engagement in a calm residential environment.

07Meals

Nutritious Home-Cooked Meals

Fresh meals prepared with attention to resident preferences, dietary needs, cultural comfort, and family/provider guidance when applicable.

08Engagement

Daily Activities & Engagement

Light exercise, music, games, crafts, conversation, and meaningful routines that support connection and emotional wellbeing.

09Home Care

Housekeeping & Laundry

Clean, organized living spaces, clothing, and linens maintained with steady attention and care.

10Environment

Safe, Comfortable Home

A peaceful home setting designed for comfort, accessibility, dignity, and family-like daily living.

11Dining

Meal Assistance & Hydration

Support for residents who need gentle help with meals or reminders to maintain healthy hydration habits.

12Comfort

Comfortable Residential Living

Residents can enjoy warm living spaces, a modern kitchen, dining area, accessible bathroom features, and outdoor patio/deck.

Specialized CNA support

Hands-on routines for safety, hygiene, mobility, and memory support.

Rahwa provides attentive CNA-led support within the adult family home setting, with care fit reviewed directly before placement.

13Mobility

Fall-Risk Prevention & Mobility Assistance

Hands-on support with transfers, walking, safer movement, and daily safety awareness.

14Hygiene

Skin Integrity & Hygiene Care

Regular hygiene routines, repositioning awareness, and comfort checks to help residents feel clean, supported, and dignified.

15Memory Support

Compassionate Dementia & Memory Care

Calm routines, patient communication, and familiar daily structure for residents experiencing cognitive decline.

16Communication

Transparent Family Updates

Open communication with families about care routines, comfort, and meaningful changes families should know about.

17Coordination

Care Coordination

Collaboration with families and outside providers such as doctors, nurses, therapists, or case managers when appropriate.

18Family Approach

Warm, Family-Centered Care

Every resident is treated with respect, patience, kindness, and the same dignity families want for their own loved ones.

Safety and comfort

Designed for Safer Daily Routines

The home includes accessible bathroom features such as grab bars and walk-in shower access to support comfort, dignity, and safer daily care.
