Privacy Policy

Information we collect

When you submit a contact or tour form, Rahwa Elderly Care may collect your name, phone number, email address, relationship to the resident, care timeline, preferred tour details, and message.

How information is used

Information is used to respond to your inquiry, coordinate tours, and communicate about care fit or room availability. Website form data may be routed to a Google Sheet through a Google Apps Script endpoint once configured.

Sharing

Rahwa Elderly Care does not sell website inquiry information. Information may be shared only when needed to respond to your request, comply with law, or operate website and lead-capture tools.
